About Neil

With over 40 years experience as a jeweller, Neil Keown specialises in custom designed and made jewellery. He works closely with his clients to ensure that what he's designing suits each customer's desires and requirements.
Neil understands that; "Giving someone you love something hand crafted that has been thought out from the smallest detail and designed with love and care is a gift from the heart."
Working in many locations and being a well sought after jeweller, Neil has now made a place for himself at his workshop in Gosford. Being located only an hour drive from Sydney, Neil has gathered a strong Sydney clientele basis, as well as being a favourite amongst the locals on the Central Coast. Neil's focus is crafting jewellery of the highest quality, using his attention to detail and only the finest materials, leaving all his clients with something they will love and cherish forever.
